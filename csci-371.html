Learning Outcomes: This course is designed to help students build confidence crafting larger, more complicated computer programs. Students will implement and experiment with fundamental techniques required to build, visualize, and animate virtual worlds. We will "play with code" and express ourselves creatively. Grading: The final semester grade is broken down as (9 - 1) * 7.5% = 60% Homework (lowest homework dropped), 20% Final Project, 10% Midterm Exam, 10% Final Exam.
